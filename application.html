Here at I FLY YOUNG CCE, we want to help you leave the ground and learn to soar. Why do we do this?
  • - We know how important a career is to a person's life journey, as we are all seasoned career professionals
  • - We believe in an ecosystem of giving and receiving
  • - We are beneficiaries of generous giving from people before us
  • - We want you to be inspired and continue the culture of giving to those after you
Focus Areas
  • "How did you find your internships? How did you overcome the catch-22 of needing prior experience, even for your first job?" These are the most commonly asked questions by frustrated job seekers, and we design the CCE internship program to address this exact problem.

  • How? We value "determination" more than "experience". As long as you meet the requirements (see each position for details), can demonstrate the eagerness to learn and commitment to quality deliverables, we will give you a chance.

  • Who is this for? Whether you are looking for a first internship, or exploring career options that better fit your talents, you are all welcome to apply.

  • While we expect more programs in the future to cover broader areas of expertise, below are the 5 focus areas that we are accepting applicants for CCE Internship: Soaring Eagle Winter 2021.
AI:

Data Science & Machine Learning Intern

Gain hands on data analysis and machine learning experience through guided step by step practices, from the introductory to the advanced.

×

Data Science & Machine Learning Internship Overview

In this specialization track, we are looking for candidates who are passionate about data science / machine learning and ready to level up their AI career through hands-on projects valued by industry leaders. We will take you through a series of training on AI fundamentals, followed by two major components. In the first component, the candidates will explore a given data set and build machine learning models by data analysis, feature engineering and model building. In the Computer Vision component, the candidates will build deep learning software for auto detection of traffic signs and signals for self-driving vehicles. Beginner or advanced, you will have a superb opportunity to acquire / strengthen your AI skills, and launch your career towards the next big thing.

Internship Objectives
  • Acquire fundamental knowledge in data science & machine learning
  • Receive training on progamming languages/packages and IDEs in AI
  • Gain insights on classic AI methods deployed by tech giants and startups
  • Catapult your growth through collaboration with the cohort
  • Unlock unlimited applications and job opportunities
Qualifications
  • Candidates with a strong drive in knowledge acquisition and self-motivation
  • Familiarity with programming language (Python, Java, R) and mathematics
  • Prior experience with data visualization & ML is a plus
  • Knowledge in Tensorflow/Pytorch/Keras, Embedded Systems and GPUs is a plus
  • Commitment to demonstrate solid progress on a bi-weekly basis for 6 months.

Sales Associate Intern

Launch your tech sales career with CCE sales Associate Internship Program to develop job-ready skill sets, expand horizons to the cutting edge innovations in cloud computing, and gain invaluable insights to career paths from industry leaders.

CCE Sales Associates Internship Program Overview

The CCE Sales Associates Internship Program is looking to recruit and develop the next generation of Tech Sales and Product Managers for our community. We are seeking talented and enthusiastic students who are passionate about Cloud Computing, Machine Learning, and Advanced Analytics and want to experiment with innovative technologies to solve complex and diverse challenges. This is an excellent opportunity to join the CCE Sales Associates Internship Program, working with some experienced industry leaders while also developing your skills and furthering your career in technology companies.

Internship Program Objectives
  • Develop technical sales skills and domain knowledge through industry-recognized certification and proven learning curriculum.
  • Experiment with real-world, on-the-job experience through automated hands-on projects, including Machine Learning and Advanced Analytics solutions with pre-built templates.
  • Exposure to a wide variety of Tech Sales and Product Manage roles and gain invaluable insights into career planning and trajectory from experienced industry leaders.
  • Build interview readiness skills, a professional portfolio, and your personal brand to stand out at every step of the hiring process with technology companies.
Basic Qualifications
  • Enrolled in a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or related fields (STEM - Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) with active senior class status.
  • Experience with one of the following computer science domains - networking fundamentals, Storage, Security, Databases (Relational and/or NoSQL), Operating Systems (Unix, Linux, and/or Windows)
  • Commitment of 6 hours per weeks for six months
Preferred Qualifications
  • Experience in implementing cloud-based solutions such as AWS, Microsoft Azure, or Google GCP in school projects.
  • Strong verbal and written communications skills; past experience writing and publishing technical papers desirable.

Full Stack Web Dev Internship Overview

There is no doubt that web skills are becoming an essential competency and a highly sought-after artistry in the tech world. Everyday, our lives revolve around web contents and social media that are crafted by gifted developers and powerful open-source software. In this part-time program, we are looking for self-motivated interns who are passionate about web development and ready to launch their ideas into real-life applications. Beginner or sophisticated, you will have the opportunity to solidify your web fundamentals and advance your skills through collaboration.

Internship Objectives
  • Acquire fundamental knowledge in full stack concepts and practices
  • Learn about popular web dev frameworks
  • Present case studies on classic web templates and design patterns
  • Catapult your growth through collaboration with the cohort
  • Unlock unlimited applications and job opportunities

  • Familiarity with programming languages and software engineering

UX Design: Front End

UX Design Intern

This project is ideal for those who are early in their UI/UX career, those who are new to the field and are trying to break in, or any UX designer who wants to understand how to create a good portfolio, and how to use story telling to showcase their work in a portfolio review.

×

UX Design Internship Program Overview

As a UX Designer, portfolios are a part of how you want to represent yourself as a brand and the quality of the work you are capable of doing. A UX portfolio is a representation of your design abilities, your design process, and how you visually represent your work to others. This project is designed to help you create a UX design portfolio and a portfolio presentation tailored to getting hired in a corporate setting.

Primary Responsibilities
  • Build and launch your UX digital portfolio/website.
  • Record a video of your portfolio presentation and launch on YouTube channel.
What Can You Learn From This Project

If you are interested in getting start in UX field, I'll give you some guidelines on what elements go into a good UX portfolio, and I'll explain how to use story telling during the portfolio review on the standard UX interview process. This project is ideal for those who are early in their UX career, those who are new to the field and are trying to break in, or any UX designer who wants to understand how to create a good portfolio, and how to use story telling to showcase their work in a portfolio review.

Qualifications
  • Commitment of 10 hours per week, 16 weeks total
  • Passionate in UX/UI, visual, product and media design
  • Has hands-on experience with UX/UI projects
  • College major in graphic design, multimedia design, interaction design, visual communication design, informatics design or equivalent majors
  • Detail-oriented and organized
  • Creative mindsets
Business Analytics: Ads

Facebook Ads Management Intern

Facebook Ads Management Internship Overview

As a Facebook Ads Manager, you will work to scale social advertising efforts geared towards growing the CCE Pro network by promoting CCE organization and events. The role is for someone who is passionate in building, maintaining and optimizing digital advertising campaigns with a good blend of creativity and an analytical mindset.

Primary Responsibilities
  • Build and launch Facebook / Instagram Ads for CCE
  • Manage and monitor Ads budget
  • Coordinate with content and UX team to create and generate the content for the Ads to be posted regularly (e.g. weekly / bi-weekly)
  • Create weekly and monthly reports of how the Ads are performing
  • Monitor and Analyze campaign performance, and recommend new strategy
What Can You Learn From This Program

If you are interested in Digital Marketing and Web Analytics, this is a great opportunity to learn how to manage advertisements on major digital marketing platforms. Analyzing campaign performance and results to drive strategy by leveraging Facebook campaign insights. This experience is transferable to other social media and there is potential to expand the scope.

Qualifications
  • Commitment of 8 hours per week for 1 year
  • Major in Business, Marketing, Applied Mathematics, Data Science, Statistics, or quantitative field
  • Passionate in marketing, social advertising, business analytics
  • Experience with using Facebook, Instagram, and social media
  • Analytical and data-driven
  • Detail-oriented and organized
  • Strong teamwork and collaboration

Why Apply?

  • - Gain valuable working experience on projects valued by the industries
  • - Learn from and mentored by industry professionals
  • - Join CCE's healthy ecosystem of growing through giving and recieving
